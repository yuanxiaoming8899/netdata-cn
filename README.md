<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">即使在复杂的多/混合/混合云环境中，它也可以很好地从一台服务器扩展到数千台服务器，并且只要有足够的磁盘空间，它就可以将您的指标保存多年。</font></font></p>
<p dir="auto"><strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">主要特征</font></font></strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">：</font></font><br></p>

操作系统指标、容器指标、虚拟机、硬件传感器、应用程序指标、OpenMetrics 导出器、StatsD 和日志。</font></font></p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">💪</font></font><strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">实时、低延迟、高分辨率</font></font></strong><br><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
所有指标每秒收集一次，并在数据收集后立即显示在仪表板上。</font><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">Netdata 的设计目标是快速。</font></font></p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">😶&zwj;🌫️</font></font><strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">无监督异常检测</font></font></strong><br><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">

</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">🔥</font></font><strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">强大的可视化</font></font></strong><br><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
清晰精确的可视化，让您快速了解任何数据集，还可以直接在仪表板上对数据进行过滤、切片和切块，无需学习任何查询语言。</font></font></p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">🔔</font></font><strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">开箱即用的警报</font></font></strong><br><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">

<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">😎</font></font><strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">低维护量</font></font></strong><br><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">

<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">⭐ </font></font><strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">Netdata 是基于 Docker 的系统最节能的监控工具</font></font></strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">⭐</font></font><br></p>
<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">2023 年 12 月 11 日：</font></font><a href="https://twitter.com/IMalavolta/status/1734208439096676680" rel="nofollow"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">阿姆斯特丹大学发表了一项</font></font></a><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">有关监控工具对基于 Docker 的系统的影响的研究，旨在回答 2 个问题：</font></font></p>
<ol dir="auto">
<li><strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">监控工具对基于 Docker 的系统的能效有何影响？</font></font></strong></li>

<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">他们在 9 种不同的配置（低、中、高频与低、中、高工作负载，3x3）下测试了 ELK、Prometheus、Netdata 和 Zipkin。</font></font></p>
<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">Netdata 的立场是这样的：</font></font></p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">🚀 Netdata 在能源效率方面表现出色：</font></font><strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">“...Netdata 是最节能的工具...”</font></font></strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">，正如研究所说。</font></font></li>
<li><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">🚀 Netdata 在 CPU 使用率、RAM 使用率和执行时间方面表现出色，并且在网络流量方面与 Prometheus 具有类似的影响。</font></font></li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">该研究没有根据收集的指标数量对结果进行标准化。</font><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">鉴于 Netdata 通常比其他工具收集更多的指标，因此 Netdata 成功地超越了其他工具，同时吸收了更多数量的指标。</font></font><a href="https://www.ivanomalavolta.com/files/papers/ICSOC_2023.pdf" rel="nofollow"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">在这里阅读完整的研究</font></font></a><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">。</font></font></p>

<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">Netdata 是围绕</font></font><strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">模块化指标处理管道</font></font></strong><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">构建的。</font></font></p>
<details><summary><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">点击查看有关此管道的更多详细信息...</font></font></summary>
&nbsp;<br>
<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">每个网络数据代理可以执行以下功能：</font></font></p>
<ol dir="auto">
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>COLLECT</code><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">来自其来源的指标</font></font></strong><br><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
使用</font></font><a href="https://github.com/netdata/netdata/tree/master/collectors"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">内部</font></font></a><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">和</font></font><a href="https://github.com/netdata/go.d.plugin/tree/master/modules"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">外部</font></font></a><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">插件从其来源收集数据。</font></font></p>
<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">Netdata 自动检测并收集操作系统中的几乎所有内容：包括 CPU、中断、内存、磁盘、挂载点、文件系统、网络堆栈、网络接口、容器、虚拟机、进程、</font></font><code>systemd</code><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">单元、Linux 性能指标、Linux eBPF、硬件传感器、IPMI 等。</font></font></p>
<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">它从以下应用程序收集应用程序指标：PostgreSQL、MySQL/MariaDB、Redis、MongoDB、Nginx、Apache 等。</font></font></p>
<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">Netdata 还通过抓取 OpenMetrics 导出器或通过 StatsD 来收集您的自定义应用程序指标。</font></font></p>
<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">它可以将 Web 服务器日志文件转换为指标，并对它们实时应用机器学习和警报。</font></font></p>
<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">它还支持综合测试/白盒测试，因此您可以 ping 服务器、检查 API 响应，甚至检查文件系统文件和目录以生成指标、训练 ML 并运行有关其状态的警报和通知。</font></font></p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>STORE</code><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">指标到数据库</font></font></strong><br><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">
使用数据库引擎插件将收集的数据存储在内存和/或磁盘上。</font><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">我们开发了自己的</font></font><a href="https://github.com/netdata/netdata/tree/master/database/engine#readme"><code>dbengine</code></a><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">数据存储方式，以非常高效的方式存储数据，使 Netdata 的磁盘上每个样本的大小少于 1 个字节，并且查询速度快得惊人。</font></font></p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>LEARN</code> the behavior of metrics</strong> (ML)<br>
Trains multiple Machine-Learning (ML) models per metric to learn the behavior of each metric individually. Netdata uses the <code>kmeans</code> algorithm and creates by default a model per metric per hour, based on the values collected for that metric over the last 6 hours. The trained models are persisted to disk.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>DETECT</code> anomalies in metrics</strong> (ML)<br>
Uses the trained machine learning (ML) models to detect outliers and mark collected samples as <strong>anomalies</strong>. Netdata stores anomaly information together with each sample and also streams it to Netdata Parents so that the anomaly is also available at query time for the whole retention of each metric.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>CHECK</code> metrics and trigger alert notifications</strong><br>
Uses its configured alerts (you can configure your own) to check the metrics for common issues and uses notifications plugins to send alert notifications.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>STREAM</code> metrics to other Netdata Agents</strong><br>
Push metrics in real-time to Netdata Parents.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>ARCHIVE</code> metrics to 3rd party databases</strong><br>
Export metrics to industry standard time-series databases, like <code>Prometheus</code>, <code>InfluxDB</code>, <code>OpenTSDB</code>, <code>Graphite</code>, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>QUERY</code> metrics and present dashboards</strong><br>
Provide an API to query the data and present interactive dashboards to users.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>SCORE</code> metrics to reveal similarities and patterns</strong><br>
Score the metrics according to the given criteria, to find the needle in the haystack.</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p dir="auto">When using Netdata Parents, all the functions of a Netdata Agent (except data collection) can be delegated to Parents to offload production systems.</p>
<p dir="auto">The core of Netdata is developed in C. We have our own <code>libnetdata</code>, that provides:</p>
<ul dir="auto">
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>DICTIONARY</code></strong><br>
A high-performance algorithm to maintain both indexed and ordered pools of structures Netdata needs. It uses JudyHS arrays for indexing, although it is modular: any hashtable or tree can be integrated into it. Despite being in C, dictionaries follow object-oriented programming principles, so there are constructors, destructors, automatic memory management, garbage collection, and more. For more see <a href="https://github.com/netdata/netdata/tree/master/libnetdata/dictionary">here</a>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>ARAL</code></strong><br>
ARray ALlocator (ARAL) is used to minimize the system allocations made by Netdata. ARAL is optimized for peak performance when multi-threaded. It also allows all structures that use it to be allocated in memory-mapped files (shared memory) instead of RAM. For more see <a href="https://github.com/netdata/netdata/tree/master/libnetdata/aral">here</a>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>PROCFILE</code></strong><br>
A high-performance <code>/proc</code> (but also any) file parser and text tokenizer. It achieves its performance by keeping files open and adjustings its buffers to read the entire file in one call (which is also required by the Linux kernel). For more see <a href="https://github.com/netdata/netdata/tree/master/libnetdata/procfile">here</a>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>STRING</code></strong><br>
A string internet mechanism, for string deduplication and indexing (using JudyHS arrays), optimized for multi-threaded usage. For more see <a href="https://github.com/netdata/netdata/tree/master/libnetdata/string">here</a>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>ARL</code></strong><br>
Adaptive Resortable List (ARL), is a very fast list iterator, that keeps the expected items on the list in the same order they are found in input list. So, the first iteration is somewhat slower, but all the following iterations are perfectly aligned for best performance. For more see <a href="https://github.com/netdata/netdata/tree/master/libnetdata/adaptive_resortable_list">here</a>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>BUFFER</code></strong><br>
A flexible text buffer management system that allows Netdata to automatically handle dynamically sized text buffer allocations. The same mechanism is used for generating consistent JSON output by the Netdata APIs. For more see <a href="https://github.com/netdata/netdata/tree/master/libnetdata/buffer">here</a>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>SPINLOCK</code></strong><br>
Like POSIX <code>MUTEX</code> and <code>RWLOCK</code> but a lot faster, based on atomic operations, with significantly smaller memory impact, while being portable.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p dir="auto"><strong><code>PGC</code></strong><br>
A caching layer that can be used to cache any kind of time-related data, with automatic indexing (based on a tree of JudyL arrays), memory management, evictions, flushing, pressure management. This is extensively used in <code>dbengine</code>. For more see <a href="https://github.com/netdata/netdata/blob/master/database/engine/README.md">here</a>.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p dir="auto">The above, and many more, allow Netdata developers to work on the application fast and with confidence. Most of the business logic in Netdata is a work of mixing the above.</p>
<p dir="auto">Netdata data collection plugins can be developed in any language. Most of our application collectors though are developed in <a href="https://github.com/netdata/go.d.plugin">Go</a>.</p>

&nbsp;<br>&nbsp;<br>
<p dir="auto">Netdata has been designed to spread disk writes across time. Each metric is flushed to disk every 17 minutes, but metrics are flushed evenly across time, at an almost constant rate. Also, metrics are packed into bigger blocks we call <code>extents</code> and are compressed with LZ4 before saving them, to minimize the number of I/O operations made.</p>
<p dir="auto">Netdata also employs direct I/O for all its database operations, ensuring optimized performance. By managing its own caches, Netdata avoids overburdening system caches, facilitating a harmonious coexistence with other applications.</p>
<p dir="auto">Single node Agents (not Parents), should have a constant rate of about 50 KiB/s or less, with some spikes above that every minute (flushing of tier 1) and higher spikes every hour (flushing of tier 2).</p>
<p dir="auto">Health Alerts and Machine-Learning run queries to evaluate their expressions and learn from the metrics' patterns. These are also spread over time, so there should be an almost constant read rate too.</p>
<p dir="auto">To make Netdata not use the disks at all, we suggest the following:</p>
<ol dir="auto">
<li>Use database mode <code>alloc</code> or <code>ram</code> to disable writing metric data to disk.</li>
<li>Configure streaming to push in real-time all metrics to a Netdata Parent. The Netdata Parent will maintain metrics on disk for this node.</li>
<li>Disable ML and health on this node. The Netdata Parent will do them for this node.</li>
<li>Use the Netdata Parent to access the dashboard.</li>
</ol>
<p dir="auto">Using the above, the Netdata Agent on your production system will not use a disk.</p>

<p dir="auto">First, we have to say that Prometheus as a time-series database and Grafana as a visualizer are excellent tools for what they do.</p>

<p dir="auto"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">借助 Netdata，您的数据始终位于本地，并且您的指标始终是高分辨率的。</font></font></p>
<details><summary><font style="vertical-align: inherit;"><font style="vertical-align: inherit;">点击查看详细解答...</font></font></summary>
&nbsp;<br>&nbsp;<br>
<p dir="auto">Most commercial monitoring providers face a significant challenge: they centralize all metrics to their infrastructure and this is, inevitably, expensive. It leads them to one or more of the following:</p>
<ol dir="auto">
<li>be unrealistically expensive</li>
<li>limit the number of metrics they collect</li>
<li>limit the resolution of the metrics they collect</li>
</ol>
<p dir="auto">As a result, they try to find a balance: collect the least possible data, but collect enough to have something useful out of it.</p>
<p dir="auto">We, at Netdata, see monitoring in a completely different way: <strong>monitoring systems should be built bottom-up and be rich in insights</strong>, so we focus on each component individually to collect, store, check and visualize everything related to each of them, and we make sure that all components are monitored. Each metric is important.</p>
<p dir="auto">This is why Netdata trains multiple machine-learning models per metric, based exclusively on their own past (no sampling of data, no sharing of trained models) to detect anomalies based on the specific use case and workload each component is used.</p>
<p dir="auto">This is also why Netdata alerts are attached to components (instances) and are configured with dynamic thresholds and rolling windows, instead of static values.</p>
<p dir="auto">The distributed nature of Netdata helps scale this approach: your data is spread inside your infrastructure, as close to the edge as possible. Netdata is not one data lane. Each Netdata Agent is a data lane and all of them together build a massive distributed metrics processing pipeline that ensures all your infrastructure components and applications are monitored and operating as they should.</p>
